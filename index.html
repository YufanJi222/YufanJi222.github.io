Selected Publications

Robust treatment assignment with uncertainty-aware causal forests: Joint optimization of accuracy and estimation uncertainty

Expert Systems with Applications, 2026

This paper introduces an uncertainty-aware causal forest framework for treatment assignment. Instead of optimizing only prediction accuracy, the method also minimizes confidence-interval width, producing treatment effect estimates that are more stable, better calibrated, and more useful for risk-aware decision-making in policy and other applied settings.

Efficiency and consumer welfare under retail electricity deregulation: Analysis of Ohio’s retail choice markets

Journal of Critical Infrastructure Policy, 2025

This paper builds a comprehensive database of daily retail electricity offers in Ohio and examines market outcomes from the consumer perspective. The study shows that a large share of competitive offers were more expensive than the default utility service, and that welfare-improving offers were available only part of the time, raising concerns about efficiency and consumer welfare in retail choice markets.
